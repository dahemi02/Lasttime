Sunset Farms, Nuwara Eliya

Located in the the hillside of Sri Lanka, Sunset farms in Nuwara Eliya has always been famous for providing fresh, organic vegetables for the people in Sri Lanka. The Farmers dedication to sustainable farming practices ensures that you get the healthiest produce possible.


Freshness Well, Bandarawela

Freshness Well farms in Bandarawela vegetable farms provides vegetables from community gardens and urban farms. We focus on and collect local, pesticide-free produce, supporting agriculture and reducing the carbon footprint associated with food transportation.


Veggies, Badulla

Situated in Uva Province of Sri Lanka, Veggies farm in Badulla specializes in hydroponically grown vegetables. Their innovative techniques allow them to produce high-quality, fresh vegetables year-round, regardless of weather conditions.
